The technical documentation for the Juku E5104 computer was found by museum staff in the
building of the former Baltijets factory in Narva. Scanned in November 2024. We thank you for your
cooperation and assistance:

   1. Allan Kaldoja
   2. Danila Sukharev
   3. Narva Museum

The Computer Museum is always open to collaboration. If you have additional information regarding
the development and production of Juku, please contact us at info@arvutimuuseum.ee
