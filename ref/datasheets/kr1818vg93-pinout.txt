Juku direct-continuity interpretation, 2026-07-15
-------------------------------------------------
D93.1 is internally NC/back-bias. On the Juku PCB its socket contact is not
unconnected: exposed front copper joins it to D94.5.
D93.2 (/WE) <-> D94.4.
D93.3 (/CS) <-> D94.15 (/CE).
D93.4 (/RE) <-> D94.3.
D93.40 (+12 V) <-> board +12 V rail.

Do not restore the superseded mirrored-photo mapping D94.1/.2/.3 to
D93.4/.3/.2.
