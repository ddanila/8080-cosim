КТ315Б output-stage reference for Juku VT2
=======================================

Board identity and package
--------------------------

The exact-revision owner photographs identify VT2 as КТ315Б, marking
"Б / 8901". The board model uses 1=E, 2=C, 3=B. The old KT-13 outline in
ref/datasheets/kt315-family-promelec.pdf shows emitter, collector, base from
left to right in its front view, matching that board mapping. A later TO-92
package variant must not be used to infer this board's physical lead order.

Exact-grade limits from the preserved family scan
-------------------------------------------------

For КТ315Б the scan states:

- DC current gain h21e = 50..350 at VCE=10 V and IC=1 mA;
- transition frequency >= 250 MHz at VCE=10 V and IC=1 mA;
- VCE(sat) <= 0.4 V at IC=20 mA and IB=2 mA;
- VBE(sat) <= 1.0 V at IC=20 mA and IB=2 mA;
- collector-junction capacitance <= 7 pF at VCB=10 V;
- continuous VCE <= 20 V with RBE=10 kOhm;
- continuous collector current <= 100 mA; and
- collector dissipation <= 150 mW at ambient <= +25 C.

Model boundary
--------------

The static X7 sweep uses 50..350 as published grade limits. The 0.4 V
VCE(sat) value is a conservative saturation-headroom threshold, even though
its data-sheet test point is well above the model's collector current. The
0.55..0.85 V active VBE range remains an engineering sensitivity range: the
published 1.0 V number is a forced-saturation limit, not an active-region VBE
curve. None of these data identify the installed transistor's actual gain.
