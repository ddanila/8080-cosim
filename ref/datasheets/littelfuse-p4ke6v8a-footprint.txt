Committed-footprint interpretation
----------------------------------

KiCad footprint: D_DO-41_SOD81_P7.62mm_Horizontal
Pad 1: local (0.0, 0.0) mm, 2.20 mm copper, 1.10 mm drill, VCC
Pad 2: local (7.62, 0.0) mm, 2.20 mm copper, 1.10 mm drill, GND
F.Fab body: 5.20 mm by 2.70 mm

The 7.62 mm pitch leaves 1.21 mm between each maximum-length body end and its
hole center. The 1.10 mm finished-hole target exceeds the 0.86 mm maximum lead
diameter by 0.24 mm. This is the standard KiCad DO-41/SOD81 7.62 mm horizontal
fit, but lead forming close to the body and first-article seating still require
physical inspection.

Polarity and role
-----------------

D1 pad 1 is the cathode on fused VCC and pad 2 is the anode on GND. The device
is therefore reverse-biased at normal positive rail voltage and avalanches on a
positive transient. Its 5.80 V stand-off exceeds the nominal 5.0 V rail.

The 10.5 V maximum clamp is specified only at the 39 A, 10/1000 us pulse point.
This part is an EFT/ESD/transient-energy shunt after F1; it is not a precision
5 V limiter and does not guarantee protection from a sustained wrong supply,
USB-C source fault, or every logic-device absolute-maximum excursion. Define
the accepted surge/ESD environment and verify rail waveforms on the first
article before treating D1 as a released protection design.
