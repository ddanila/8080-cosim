D53 КР531ИД7 / SN54S138 timing reference
==========================================

Board facts
-----------

The exact-revision Juku sheet and board model identify D53 as КР531ИД7. The
traced package contract is the 16-pin 3-to-8 active-low decoder arrangement:
A/B/C on pins 1/2/3, active-high G1 on pin 6, active-low G2A/G2B on pins 4/5,
and Y0..Y7 on pins 15/14/13/12/11/10/9/7. On Juku, Y0..Y3 feed R49..R52 and
the four RAS rails; Y4..Y7 are source-proved no-connects.

Primary compatible-device timing reference
------------------------------------------

Texas Instruments SN54S138 is the same Schottky-TTL 3-to-8 decoder function
and pin arrangement used as the closest manufacturer timing reference. The
preserved PDF is `sn54s138-ti.pdf`, TI document SDLS014 (December 1972,
revised March 1988), retrieved 2026-07-22:

  SHA256 9c33e08a3bfb7ab3b685848eee0d80457774918ce0bd3224e17cd0c1970a20a9
  https://www.ti.com/lit/ds/symlink/sn54s138.pdf

The SN54S138 switching table states VCC=5 V, TA=25 C, RL=280 ohm, CL=15 pF:

  path                    delay  typical  maximum
  binary select, 2 levels tPLH     4.5 ns    7 ns
  binary select, 2 levels tPHL     7 ns     10.5 ns
  binary select, 3 levels tPLH     7.5 ns   12 ns
  binary select, 3 levels tPHL     8 ns     12 ns
  enable, 2 levels        tPLH     5 ns      9 ns
  enable, 2 levels        tPHL     7 ns     11 ns
  enable, 3 levels        tPLH     7 ns     11 ns
  enable, 3 levels        tPHL     7 ns     11 ns

The largest published compatible-device value at that test point is 12 ns.

Evidence boundary
-----------------

This is a primary manufacturer limit for the compatible SN54S138, not an
exact КР531ИД7 production guarantee. It bounds the expected order of magnitude
and prevents a zero-context timing assumption, but it does not prove Soviet
process corners or the populated part's behavior.

It also does not close the Juku-specific D53 G1/G2 enable origins, the
VID_CPU_SEL/slot schedule, the D36 CAS-input source, board loading, or the
physical RAS pulse widths. Therefore the untimed structural HDL must not gain
invented `#delay` values from this reference. Use the 12 ns figure only as a
compatible-device comparison until an exact КР531ИД7 manufacturer sheet and
board measurements are available.
