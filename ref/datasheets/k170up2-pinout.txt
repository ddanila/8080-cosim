К170УП2 pinout interpretation — Juku D104
============================================================

Primary artifact: k170up2.pdf
Package: 16 pins
Function: four line receivers/amplifiers

Datasheet pin contract
----------------------
1,2,3,14  threshold-control inputs
4         X1 input
5         X2 input
6         X3 input
7         X4 input
8         common / GND
9         unused
10        X4 output
11        X3 output
12        X2 output
13        X1 output
15        +5 V supply
16        +12 V supply

Channel pairs: 4->13, 5->12, 6->11, 7->10.

Juku interpretation and evidence
--------------------------------
D104.4->D104.13 = serial SIN receiver.
D104.5->D104.12 = serial CTS receiver.
D104.6->D104.11 = serial DSR receiver.
D104.7 is the fourth receiver input, not NC.

Owner resistance recheck, 2026-07-19:
  D104.7 measures approximately 84 kohm to D94.13 and is not the same net.
  D94.13 is instead owner-closed to D105.3 qualified peripheral /WR;
  D104.7 remains an independent receiver-input boundary.

Owner continuity plus the exact-revision drawing, 2026-07-21, closes D104.10
as NC. Threshold pins 1/2/3/14 and the physical +12 V feed at pin16 still
require board-level disposition. Do not infer those connections solely from
the datasheet.
