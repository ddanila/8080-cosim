К170УП2 pinout interpretation — Juku D104
============================================================

Primary artifact: k170up2.pdf
Package: 16 pins
Function: four line receivers/amplifiers

Datasheet pin contract
----------------------
1,2,3,14  threshold-control inputs
4         X1 input
5         X2 input
6         X3 input
7         X4 input
8         common / GND
9         unused
10        X4 output
11        X3 output
12        X2 output
13        X1 output
15        +5 V supply
16        +12 V supply

Channel pairs: 4->13, 5->12, 6->11, 7->10.

Juku interpretation and evidence
--------------------------------
D104.4->D104.13 = serial SIN receiver.
D104.5->D104.12 = serial CTS receiver.
D104.6->D104.11 = serial DSR receiver.
D104.7 is the fourth receiver input, not NC.

Direct owner continuity, 2026-07-15:
  D104.7 <-> D94.13 <-> unidentified pull-up resistor -> +5 V.

D104.10, threshold pins 1/2/3/14, and the physical +12 V feed at pin16
still require board-level disposition. Do not infer those connections solely
from the datasheet.
