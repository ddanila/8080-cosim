Committed-footprint interpretation
----------------------------------

KiCad footprint: Fuse_Bourns_MF-RG300
Pad 1: local (0.0, 0.0) mm, 2.01 mm copper, 1.01 mm drill, VCC_RAW
Pad 2: local (5.1, 1.2) mm, 2.01 mm copper, 1.01 mm drill, VCC
Pad-centre separation: 5.239 mm
F.Fab body: 7.1 mm by 3.0 mm

The stock KiCad footprint's 5.1 mm horizontal span matches dimension C, but it
also staggers pad 2 by an undimensioned 1.2 mm. The resulting hole-centre
separation is 5.239 mm. The 1.01 mm drill exceeds the 0.81 mm nominal lead
diameter by 0.20 mm, and the kinked radial leads remain formable. This is a
static fit disposition, not a claim that dimension C's tolerance authorizes an
orthogonal stagger or that the footprint coordinates duplicate the drawing.
Inspect insertion, body seating, solder fillets, and thermal clearance on the
first assembled article.

Electrical disposition
----------------------

Rev-A F1 is wired VCC_RAW -> F1 -> VCC. At the 23 C data-sheet point, 3.0 A
hold current is 1.66 times the 1.81 A conservative planning load. It falls to
2.6 A (1.44 times) at 40 C and 2.1 A (1.16 times) at 60 C. This makes the part
eligible for gross short and wiring-fault protection, not precision current
limiting. Final ambient rise, trace and connector heating, source capability,
and first-article behavior remain order-time/bench checks.
