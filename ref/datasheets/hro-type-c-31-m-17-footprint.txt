HRO TYPE-C-31-M-17 USB-C receptacle — footprint interpretation
================================================================

Scope
-----
This is a compact transcription of the checksum-pinned HRO manufacturer
drawing in `hro-type-c-31-m-17.pdf`.  It records only the facts used to accept
Rev-A J3.  The drawing remains the authority.

Exact part
----------
Manufacturer: HRO Electronics
Part number: TYPE-C-31-M-17
Construction: right-angle, surface-mount, six-contact USB Type-C receptacle
Body: 8.94 mm wide x 6.80 mm deep x 3.16 mm high
Rated load: DC 20 V, 5 A

Rev-A use
---------
J3 is power-only.  A9/B9 feed VCC_RAW, A12/B12 and all shell tabs are GND,
and A5/B5 each use a separate 5.1 kohm Rd pulldown to GND.  The six-contact
part has no USB data contacts.  Its 5 A connector rating is not evidence that
an attached USB-C source will offer the board's full planning current.
