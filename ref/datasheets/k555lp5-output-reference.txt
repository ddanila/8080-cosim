К555ЛП5 output-stage references for Juku D34
==========================================

Board identity
--------------

The exact-revision ДГШ5.109.009 ПЭЗ census identifies D34 as К555ЛП5.
This is guarded independently by ref/juku-official-009-ic-census.json and
scripts/report_official_009_ic_census.py.

Exact-device reference
----------------------

ref/datasheets/k555lp5-eandc.pdf describes К555ЛП5, К555ЛП5В, and
КМ555ЛП5. For К555ЛП5 it gives a 5 V +/-5% supply, VOL <= 0.5 V,
VOH >= 2.7 V, low input current <= 0.8 mA magnitude, high input current
<= 40 uA, fanout 10, and propagation-delay limits of 17/22 ns on and
23/30 ns off for its two stated input conditions.

The sheet does not state output-current test conditions beside its VOH/VOL
limits. However, under the standard meaning of fanout, its fanout of 10 and
per-input current limits imply full-fanout loads of 10 * 40 uA = 0.4 mA sourced
at high level and 10 * 0.8 mA = 8 mA sunk at low level. Those two current values
are derived, not verbatim data-sheet test conditions. The sheet contains no
output I/V curves, so it still cannot supply a nonlinear loaded driver.

Independent LS-TTL comparison
-----------------------------

ref/datasheets/sn74ls86a-ti.pdf is Texas Instruments SDLS124, "SN5486,
SN54LS86A, SN54S86, SN7486, SN74LS86A, SN74S86 -- Quadruple 2-Input
Exclusive-OR Gates", December 1972, revised March 1988. The relevant
SN74LS86A table is on document page 4.

At VCC=5 V and TA=25 C the table gives typical VOH=3.4 V at IOH=-0.4 mA.
Across the recommended commercial operating range it gives VOH >= 2.7 V at
IOH=-0.4 mA, VOL <= 0.4 V at IOL=4 mA, and VOL <= 0.5 V at IOL=8 mA. The
recommended output-current conditions are IOH=-0.4 mA and IOL=8 mA.

Model boundary
--------------

The exact К555ЛП5 sheet and the independent SN74LS86A sheet agree on the
2.7 V/0.5 V logic-level envelope. The TI sheet also independently corroborates
the exact sheet's fanout-derived 0.4 mA source and 8 mA sink loads. Exceeding
those loads flags that a fixed 3.4 V source is outside the exact device's stated
same-family fanout envelope. It does not reveal how far the physical pin voltage
droops or define a general К555ЛП5 acceptance curve.

Neither sheet defines the installed D34 nonlinear output impedance. Physical
X7 voltages still require a better К555ЛП5 curve source or hardware measurement.
