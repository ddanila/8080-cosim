D94.1 connects through R8, measured approximately 2 kohm, to +5 V. No other
trace or branch was observed. Full-resolution visual inspection shows D94.5
is NC. D93.1 alone owns the short trace ending at the visible gap.
All five address inputs are owner-closed. Owner continuity plus the exact
revision drawing closes output pins 6/7/9 as NC; pin 5 is likewise NC.
The remaining physical boundaries are the upstream source of the shared
enable at D94.15/D93.3 and whether D94.1 has a hidden load beyond R8.
Keep programmed-output semantics separate from physical destinations.
